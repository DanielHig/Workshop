Notes

Data Storage

All quotes are saved in your browser's local storage. Use the Export JSON button to back up or transfer quotes between devices.

Fleet & Customers

Fleet and customer registers are shared between both tools — add a vehicle once and it appears in both.

Numbering

Quote numbers are auto-generated by month: QT-202506-001. Accident and workshop quotes use separate sequences.

Exports

Each tool exports to PDF (via jsPDF), Word .docx and Excel .xlsx. PDF and Word respect your current GST/excess settings.
